PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

Benefits

₹6,000 per year in three equal installments of ₹2,000 each
Direct Benefit Transfer to registered bank accounts
No middlemen - Amount credited directly to beneficiaries

Eligibility

All landholding farmer families with cultivable land
Valid Aadhaar number and bank account details required
Institutional landholders and retired pensioners not eligible

Required Documents

Aadhaar Card
Land Ownership Documents
Citizenship Proof
Bank Account Details

Application Process

1
Visit nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or Agriculture Department office
2
Fill Form with personal, land and bank details
3
Submit documents for verification
4
Receive confirmation SMS upon approval
