Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to apply for a loan?

Typically you'll need your Aadhaar card, land ownership documents (or lease agreement), bank statements from the last 6 months, and recent passport-sized photographs. The exact requirements may vary slightly depending on the loan type.

How long does the approval process take?

For microloans, approval can be as quick as 24 hours. For larger loans like equipment financing, the process typically takes 3-5 working days after we receive all your documents.

Can I prepay my loan without penalties?

Yes! We encourage early repayment and don't charge any prepayment penalties on any of our agricultural loan products.

What if I have a bad credit history?

We take a holistic view of your application. Even with past credit issues, we may still approve your loan if you can demonstrate current farming income and provide appropriate collateral or guarantors.
